About Us
Introduction

Founded by industry veterans with a wealth of global exposure, our company brings a refreshing approach to IT industry.

Despite being an young startup, we proudly possess a staggering 600 man-years of combined expertise, a testament to our team's depth of knowledge.

What sets us apart is the fusion of technology and domain-specific insights, empowering us to deliver a comprehensive range of services across the spectrum – from consulting to seamless managed services. Our reach knows no bounds, as we currently cater to a diverse clientele scattered across geographies.

We take immense pride in serving as a strategic IT partner to major global companies, shaping the technological landscape of tomorrow.

Our Guiding Principles
Vision

To be the leading digital services organization that generates value for our customers by enhancing the work life balance of their digital community/users through automation, simplification and humanization of their experience with innovative IT applications.

Ethos

We are an Employee driven Customer centric organization focussed on value generation for customers. Fuelled with Passion, Agility and zeal for Innovation, we deliver exceptional performance though collective contribution.
